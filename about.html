About the Project

What is
Eureka?

An AI-powered field research companion for biology students and nature enthusiasts — built to make species identification fast, accurate, and academically useful.

The Mission

Built for the Field,
Designed for the Lab.

"Eureka was created with one goal — to bridge the gap between field observation and academic documentation. Carrying a field guide isn't always practical. Having a trained AI in your pocket is."

Traditional species identification requires either years of expertise or bulky reference materials. Eureka replaces both with a simple photograph. Point, capture, and receive instant scientific-grade identification complete with taxonomy, habitat data, and GPS-tagged records.

Every sighting is automatically saved to a personal field log that can be exported for academic submissions, research papers, or biodiversity surveys.

How It Works

From Photo to
Scientific Record.

01

Capture or Upload

Use your device camera to photograph any plant or animal in the wild, or upload an existing image from your gallery.

02

AI Analysis

The image is sent to Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash model which analyses visual features — leaf structure, colour patterns, body morphology — to identify the species.

03

Scientific Output

Results include the common name, scientific name, taxonomic classification (family or class), habitat keywords, and a descriptive paragraph.

04

Geo-Tagged Log

Every identification is automatically saved to your personal Field Log with a GPS coordinate timestamp, ready to export as CSV or Excel.

Technology Stack

What Powers
Eureka.

🤖

Google Gemini AI

Gemini 2.0 Flash for vision-based species identification with multi-model fallback.

🐍

Python / Flask

Lightweight backend REST API handling image processing and Gemini API communication.

🔥

Firebase

Google Firebase handles authentication (Email + Google OAuth) and Firestore cloud database.

🌐

Vanilla JS

Zero-framework frontend — pure HTML, CSS and ES6 modules for fast load times on any device.

📍

Geolocation API

Browser-native GPS coordinates automatically attached to every observation record.

📊

SheetJS / CSV

One-click export of field logs to Excel or CSV for academic and research submissions.
